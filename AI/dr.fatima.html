About Me

Meet Dr. Sophia Bennett, MD, FACC

Dr. Sophia Bennett is a board-certified cardiologist and Fellow of the American College of Cardiology (FACC) with over 15 years of clinical experience. She specializes in preventive cardiology, heart failure management, and women's heart health — a field she is deeply passionate about, as cardiovascular disease remains the leading cause of death in women worldwide.

Her patient-first philosophy ensures every individual receives personalized, compassionate care backed by the latest evidence-based medicine. Dr. Bennett believes in educating her patients to be active partners in their own heart health journey.

What I Offer

Comprehensive Cardiac Services

From preventive screenings to advanced cardiac interventions, I provide a full spectrum of heart care services tailored to your individual needs.

Preventive Cardiology

Personalized risk assessments, lifestyle counseling, and early detection strategies to prevent heart disease before it starts.

Echocardiography

Advanced ultrasound imaging of your heart to evaluate structure, function, and blood flow with high-resolution precision.

Heart Failure Management

Comprehensive care programs for heart failure patients, including medication optimization, device therapy, and close monitoring.

Stress Testing

Exercise and pharmacological stress tests to evaluate coronary artery disease and assess cardiac performance under pressure.

Women's Heart Health

Specialized cardiac care addressing the unique cardiovascular risks women face, including pregnancy-related heart conditions.

Holter Monitoring

24–72 hour continuous ECG monitoring to detect arrhythmias and irregular heart rhythms in your daily environment.
